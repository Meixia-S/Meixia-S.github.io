Passions

As I have entered the young adult stage of my life there where two distinct topics that really drew my attention. The first being web development/design and the second one being evolution. I will go into more detail as to what excatly makes those two very different interest become my passion.

I hope the layout and small details of this website (when finished) can in part show that web development is something I am truly passionate about.

Both my grandmother and mother have been very artisitic and distilled it's value in me when I was younger. At that time the more traditional way of creating art never appealed to me. It was almost a bit too open ended for me and if I made mistake, it often meant I would have to start over as arcylic paint is not the most forgiving medium. Web design on the other hand is quite the oppsite. One can simply remove a line of code or go back to an older version of the site to undo any mistakes. It is a more forgiving process in that aspect. It also mixes coding into the mix that makes it a more modern art form. Being in my early 20s this is an undienable plus since it makes me feel like my work can be appercaited by my peers and has real utility to it.

The second passion of mine is less techinally driven interest. Taking ecology and biology classes in high school made me look at the natrual world with a more critical eye. Instead of thinking an animal looked odd or bazzar, I started to wonder why they looked the way they did and if it was the environment or its place in the food chain. As I got older this curisoity turned to why humans are the way we are and our evolutionary journey and its place in the current time period. As more information closly gets uncovered and piece of our past has been put together it really presents an intriguing story of where we came from as a speicies. Learning about this kind of science has really helped me put things into perspective and feel more connected with the world. I know these are quite __ statements but I think someone's passion/hobby should help people feel more present and connected to their spot in the world and for me, learning about "life's" past does that for me.

On a lighter note if you ever learn more about bugs, you'll find that they truly are the aliens that roam along side us!
